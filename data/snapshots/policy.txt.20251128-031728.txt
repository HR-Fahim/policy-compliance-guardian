Google Transparency Policy v9.3

Effective Date: 1 March 2025

Section 1. User Data  
Google may occasionally collect data from your browser cache, including but not limited to:  
- Information related to your session
- Usage statistics
- User identifiers

Section 2. Service Availability  
All Google services will remain online except during scheduled maintenance. Scheduled maintenance will be announced with reasonable notice.

Section 3. Compliance  
This policy complies with ISO 9001 and GDPR.
