GitHub Acceptable Use Policy

Short Note:
We host many projects and teams, and everyone should interact kindly. When using the Service, please follow these Policies.

All terms that are uppercased but not defined here will have the meanings they have in the Main Agreement or elsewhere. "We", "Us", and "Ourselves" refer to GitHub.

1. Complying With Laws and Regulations

You are responsible for using the Service within all applicable laws, regulations, and local rules. These policy documents may be updated periodically.

2. User Safety

Users may not post, upload, distribute, transmit, broadcast, store, or hyper-embed content that includes, but is not limited to, the following:

Content that is unlawful or contradicts any ordinance;

Sexually explicit material or any content involving non-consensual sexual content or child sexual abuse material;

Anything that is libelous, defamatory, fraudulent, or factually incorrect;

Discrimination or abusive content toward individuals, identities, groups, or clusters;

False or inaccurate information that may influence public interest or elections;

Harassment of users, non-users, employees, contractors, or automated agents;

Threats of violence or implications thereof, regardless of target;

Glorification of violence;

Spam, or any digital behavior that causes annoyance, lag, mis-rendering, or otherwise degrades platform enjoyment.
