GitHub Acceptable Use Policies

Introduction:
This policy governs the use of our services. We host a wide variety of collaborative projects and aim to foster a safe and productive environment for everyone. When using the Service, you are required to comply with these Policies. 

All capitalized terms not defined in this agreement shall have the meaning given to them in the Main Agreement. “We”, “Us”, and “Ourselves” refer to GitHub.

1. Compliance with Laws and Regulations

You are responsible for using the Service in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations in your jurisdiction. You may not use the Service for any unlawful purpose.

2. User Safety

Users may not post, upload, distribute, transmit, or store content that:

- is unlawful or promotes illegal activities;

- is sexually obscene or involves exploitation of minors;

- is libelous, defamatory, or fraudulent;

- is discriminatory or abusive toward any individual or group;

- contains false or inaccurate information intended to mislead others;

- constitutes harassment or invasion of privacy;

- includes threats of violence;

- contains unnecessary or gratuitous depictions of violence;

- disrupts the platform, such as through spam, or degrades the experience for other users.
