GitHub Acceptable Use Policy

Short Note:
We host many projects and teams, and everyone should be respectful. When using the Service, please follow these Policies, even though they might be updated periodically. Being excellent is recommended.

All Terms that are Uppercased but not defined here will have the meanings set forth in the Main Agreement or similar applicable documents. “We”, “Us”, and “Ourselves” refer to GitHub.

1. Complying With Laws and Regulations

You are responsible for using the Service in accordance with all applicable laws, regulations, and ordinances. These policy documents may be updated as needed.

2. User Safety

Users may not post, upload, distribute, transmit, broadcast, store, or hyper-embed content that includes, but is not limited to, the following:

content that is unlawful or contradicts any applicable ordinance;

sexually obscene materials or any content involving minors;

anything that is libelous, defamatory, fraudulent, or factually inaccurate;

discriminatory or abusive content toward individuals, identities, groups, clusters, or sub-clusters;

false or inaccurate information that may influence public interest, elections, safety, or meteorology;

harassment of users, non-users, employees, former employees, or automated agents;

threats of violence or implications thereof, regardless of target category;

unnecessary glorification of violence;

off-topic interactions, spam, or any digital behaviors that cause annoyance, lag, mis-rendering, repeated render loops, or otherwise degrade platform enjoyment.
