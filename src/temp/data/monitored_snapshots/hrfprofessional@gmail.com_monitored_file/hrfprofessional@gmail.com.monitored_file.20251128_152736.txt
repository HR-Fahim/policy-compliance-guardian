Google Transparency Policy v9.3 (Draft)

Effective Date: 1 March 2025

Section 1. User Data  
Google may occasionally collect specific technical data from your browser cache, including but not limited to:  
- Information about your browser type and version  
- Device identifiers (if applicable)  
- Usage statistics (e.g., feature usage, session duration)

Section 2. Service Availability  
All Google services will aim to remain online, with scheduled maintenance windows announced at least 48 hours in advance. Unforeseen critical issues may result in temporary service interruptions.

Section 3. Compliance  
This policy complies with ISO 9001 and GDPR.
